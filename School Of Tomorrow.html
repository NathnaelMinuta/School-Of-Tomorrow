.....School of tomorrow is one of the earliest and prominent private school schools in Ethiopia.

.....It was established in September 1993. Currently the school has 11 branches located in diffrent Strategic locations in Addis Ababa City. The school provides quality education for children from 2.5 to 18 years old in its nursery, elementary, secondary and pre-college levels. It serves as a good model to many. In the last 25 years the school has enormously progressed the quality of the service it provides and has increased its accessibility across the city.
