School of Tomorrow has around 11 branches. And ever branches has their own characterstics and identity. And now we will try to see every branches with their uniqe identity.

SOT CMC

CMC is the largest of all the branches in SOT. It was established in September 1999. The location is on the Road from Megenagna to Ayat, situated around Meri. It has three divisions, which are:-
Branch Division
CMC KG Division
CMC Elementary Division
CMC High School & Preparatory levels

SOT BOLE

The bole branch is located next to Addis Cardiac Hospital, on the opposite side of Ethiopian Airlines Cargo Services Department in Bole. It was established in 2008 it provides education from Nursery class up to grade 12.

It Comprises three divisions, which are:-
Branch Division
BOLE KG Division
BOLE Elementary Division
BOLE High School & Preparatory levels

SOT HAYAHULET

Hayahulete branch of SOT was established in September 1993. It is located off the street from 22 Mazoria to Bole Medhanialem Church midway close to St.Gabriel Hospital. It provides education services from Nursery to Prep class.

"This is the first Branch of School of Tommorow."

SOT BISRATE

Bisrate is found in Old Airport close to Bisrate Gabriel Church. It was established in september 2005. It now provides education in its kindergarten and Elementary Divisions.

SOT GOFA

The Gofa branch was established in September 2018. It is located in front of Gofa MebratHail Condominium and it comprises of Kindergarten and Elementary Divisions.

SOT GULELE

Gulele Branch located right next to St.Paul Hospital, started work in September 2005 and currently provides educational services comprising of elementary, High School and Preparatory Levels.

SOT LEM

Lem Branch is located around lem Hotel on Haile Gebresillassie Road. It started educational services in September 1994. It operates Elementary School from Grade 1 to 8.

SOT MEGA

Mega Branch is locatated off the road from Meskel Square to Bole International Airport Close to Mega Building. It commenced service in 2005, oprerating from Nursery to Prep.

SOT MEKANISA

Mekanisa Branch is located near the German roundabout and commenced providing educational services in September 2017. It compraises of High school and Preparatory Levels.

SOT RAS DESTA

Ras Desta Branch is located around Ras Desta Hospital close to Kidus Yohannes Church. It was established in 2015, operating in Kindergarten & Elementary Divisions.
